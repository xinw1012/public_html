I am Xin Wang, a Ph.D. student at UC Berkeley, working with Prof. Joseph E. Gonzalez and Prof. Trevor Darrell. I am part of the BAIR Lab, RISE Lab, and BDD Lab. Prior to UC Berkeley, I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University .

My research interest lies in computer vision and machine learning including few-shot learning, semi-/self-supervised learning, continual learning, efficient inference, and model serving systems with various applications in autonomous driving and robotics.

I'm currently on the faculty and industry research job market (2020-2021)!
